Maharashtra

Maharashtra is one of the largest and most developed states in India. Its capital is Mumbai, which is known as the financial capital of India. Maharashtra is famous for its rich history, culture, forts, beaches, and tourist attractions such as the Gateway of India, Ajanta Caves, Ellora Caves, and Shirdi.

The official language of Maharashtra is Marathi. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is one of the most respected historical figures associated with Maharashtra.
